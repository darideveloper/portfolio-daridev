## Essential Elements of a High-Performing Law Firm Website

To rank on Google, appear in AI-generated results, and convert visitors into clients, your site must be strategically structured.

### Practice Area Pages

Dedicated pages for each specialty (criminal law, family law, corporate law, civil litigation, etc.) help Google index your firm by **legal intent**, not just by name.

### Attorney Profiles & About Page

Humanize your firm. Credentials, certifications, and professional experience build immediate credibility and emotional trust.

### Testimonials and Case Results

Social proof is one of the strongest conversion drivers in the legal sector. When allowed by regulation, anonymized case results and verified testimonials dramatically increase inquiries.
