## Security as a Core Design Principle  
*(Applied to Legal and Medical Websites)*

Security is not an add-on. **It is part of the design itself.**

### Legal Sector Example: Authority and Confidentiality

For modern law firms, a website must balance accessibility with absolute confidentiality.

**Key elements include:**
- Encrypted communication channels
- Secure contact forms
- Clear privacy policies
- Client-only access areas when required

Security must be **visible**, not just implied.

**Result:**  
Higher-quality leads from corporate and private clients who demand discretion.

### Medical Sector Example: Ethics, Empathy, and Data Protection

In healthcare, design must reduce patient anxiety while ensuring strict data protection compliance (HIPAA, GDPR, or regional equivalents).

**Key elements include:**
- Secure appointment booking systems
- Protected medical contact forms
- Real, professional imagery of medical staff
- Clear, human-centered language

**Result:**  
Higher conversion rates from visitors to scheduled patients and reduced administrative friction.
