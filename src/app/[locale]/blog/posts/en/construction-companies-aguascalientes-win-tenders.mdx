## How to Structure a Winning Construction Portfolio

To move from being just another option to being the preferred choice, your portfolio must meet certain visual and technical quality standards.

### High-Impact Visual Documentation

Blurry photos of unfinished grey work do not sell. You need professional photography that captures:

* Before and after shots of the site.
* Details of finishes and materials.
* Machinery in action (demonstrating installed capacity).

### Accessible Technical Sheets

Every project on your website should include:

* Location and year of execution.
* Technical challenges resolved.
* Delivery time and budget (if public).
