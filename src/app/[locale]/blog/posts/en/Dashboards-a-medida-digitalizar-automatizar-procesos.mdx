## What Is a Custom Dashboard and Why Is It Better Than Excel?

A custom dashboard is a visual interface designed specifically around your **real business KPIs**.

Unlike Excel or generic SaaS tools, custom development:

- **Adapts to your workflow**, not the other way around.
- **Centralizes disconnected systems**, including CRM, billing, sales, and marketing.
- **Eliminates human error** through automated data collection.

Excel shows numbers.  
Dashboards **explain what’s happening**.
