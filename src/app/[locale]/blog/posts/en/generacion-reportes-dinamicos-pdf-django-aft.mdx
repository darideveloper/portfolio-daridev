### Rendering Tools Comparison

#### WeasyPrint vs ReportLab: Which One Should You Use?

- **WeasyPrint:** Renders HTML/CSS into PDFs. Ideal for frontend-oriented teams and rapid design iteration.
- **ReportLab:** Generates PDFs programmatically with Python. Offers absolute control and better performance at scale.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Is it hard to maintain a Django reporting system?
No, if the PDF logic is properly decoupled from application views.

### Can I use modern CSS in PDFs?
Yes. WeasyPrint supports most modern CSS standards including Flexbox and Grid.

### What if reports take too long to generate?
Move generation to asynchronous task queues and notify users when ready.

### Do these libraries require commercial licenses?
