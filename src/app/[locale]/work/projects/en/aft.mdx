## Project Description  

**AFT** is a **technology skills assessment platform** designed to help organizations measure and improve the abilities of their employees.  
The goal is to provide a digital system that facilitates decision-making in **training, professional development, and talent management**.  

In this iteration, the project focuses on the **dynamic PDF reporting module**, which automatically transforms evaluation responses into visual, easy-to-read reports. These reports allow companies to gain a clear and actionable view of their team’s technology skill levels.  

---  

## **Key Features**  

* **Dynamic PDF reports:** Automatic creation of customized documents based on evaluation responses.  
* **Data visualization:** Charts and statistics generated with **matplotlib** and **scipy** to simplify analysis.  
* **Professional report design:** Implemented with **reportlab** to ensure a clean, modern, and business-ready format.  
* **Secure cloud storage:** File management through **Amazon S3**, allowing access to reports anytime.  
* **User and evaluation management:** Built on **Django**, ensuring stability, security, and scalability.  
* **Enhanced admin experience:** Customized control panel with **Jazzmin** for simplified management.  

---  

## **Technologies Used**  

### **Backend**  
* **Django 4.2:** Main framework of the project.  
* **Django Rest Framework (DRF):** Robust API for managing evaluations and reports.  
* **PostgreSQL:** Reliable database optimized for large volumes of data.  

### **Report Generation**  
* **ReportLab and PyPDF2:** Creation and manipulation of PDF documents.  
* **Matplotlib, Numpy, and SciPy:** Data analysis and generation of statistical charts.  

### **Storage & Media**  
* **Amazon S3 + django-storages + boto3:** Cloud-based management of files and reports.  
* **Pillow:** Image processing within reports.  

### **Interface & Administration**  
* **Django Jazzmin:** Modern and easy-to-use admin panel.  

### **Testing & Quality Assurance**  
* **Selenium, BeautifulSoup, and Playwright:** Automated testing to validate functionality and performance.  

---  

## **Challenges and Learnings**  

* **Report automation:** Building a system that automatically converts responses into PDF documents with clear, visual information.  
* **Presentation of complex data:** Integration of dynamic charts to simplify result interpretation.  
* **Scalability:** Preparing the architecture to grow with the organization’s future needs.  
* **Usability for administrators:** Optimizing the management interface for fast and intuitive report generation.  

---  

## **Results**  

The **AFT** project provides companies with a practical and effective tool to **assess technology skills and make data-driven decisions**.  
The PDF reporting module turns evaluations into **clear, professional, and ready-to-use reports**, making it easier for executives, HR managers, and trainers to interpret results.  

With this solution, organizations can **identify areas for improvement, plan training strategies, and strengthen internal talent**, moving toward a more modern and future-ready human resources management approach.
