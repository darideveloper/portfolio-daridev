## Project Description

**Mexico Real** is a web project we developed for a client in the **_food_** sector, specifically a family-owned **_Mexican restaurant_** in Colorado. This **_informative website_** is designed to **highlight the authenticity of their cuisine** and **facilitate contact** with their different locations. The site aims to **attract customers** seeking a **_genuine Mexican culinary experience_**, emphasizing **flavor**, **fresh ingredients**, and **family tradition**. This project demonstrates our ability to create an **attractive and functional online presence** for **_local gastronomy_** businesses with multiple branches.

- **Presentation of the gastronomic offer:** It showcases a variety of popular Mexican dishes such as **_tacos, burritos, margaritas, and enchiladas_**, inviting users to explore the **_full menu_**.
- **Clear branch information:** It provides **addresses and contact options** (**_"ORDER NOW"_** and **_"FULL MENU"_**) for each of their three locations in Colorado Springs (**_Nevada Ave, Maizeland Road, Sinton Road_**).
- **"About Us" section:** It highlights the **_family tradition_**, the **passion for authentic cuisine**, and the use of the **_best and freshest ingredients_**.
- **Social media integration:** It incorporates an **_Instagram feed_** (**mexicorealfamilyrestaurant**) to visually display the **food**, the **atmosphere**, and the **connection with the community**.
- **Diverse contact options:** It offers a **_contact form_**, a **phone number**, and an **email address** for inquiries and feedback.
- **Practical information:** It includes **_detailed opening hours_** for each day of the week.
- **Reservation form:** It makes it easy for customers to **_book a table online_**.

## Technologies Used

- **Wordpress:** CMS for content management.
- **Divi:** Page builder for visual design.
- **Smash Balloon**: Plugin to integrate the **_Instagram feed_**.
- **Simply Schedule Appointments**: Plugin to manage the **reservation form**.
- **DariDev Contact Form API**: Team API, to create **contact forms**.

## Challenges and Learnings

- **Presenting the authenticity of Mexican cuisine:** The challenge was to communicate the **_quality and genuine flavor_** of the Mexican food to attract an audience looking for **authentic culinary experiences**.
- **Organizing information for multiple branches:** Ensuring that the contact information and menus for each location are **_easily accessible and distinguishable_** for users.
- **Integrating attractive visual elements:** Utilizing the **_Instagram feed_** to showcase the **food in an appealing way** and convey the **family-friendly and welcoming atmosphere** of the restaurant.
- **Facilitating customer interaction:** Implementing **_multiple contact methods_** (phone, email, form, social media, and reservations) to adapt to user preferences.
- **Designing intuitive navigation:** Ensuring that visitors can easily find the **menu**, the **nearest location**, **contact information**, and how to make a **reservation**.

## Results

At Mexico Real, our focus on an **_attractive_** design and a clear presentation of the **gastronomic offer** and **contact information** resulted in an **effective** website for connecting with local customers. We successfully highlighted the **_tradition and flavor of Mexico_**, making it easy for users to explore the menu, locate branches, and find different ways to **_interact with the restaurant_**. The integration of the **_Instagram feed_** adds a dynamic visual component that **reinforces the restaurant's identity**.
